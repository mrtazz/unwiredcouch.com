I liked a lot how the book always came back to talking to the kids and empathizing with their emotions instead of trying to abolish them. And there were always good examples that came with every section to illustrate the point. But there was hardly anything in there about how to deal with situations where time is tight or there isn’t the space to slow down and prolong the situation.
