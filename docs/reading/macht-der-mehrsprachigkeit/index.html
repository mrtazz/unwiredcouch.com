  • Die Macht der Mehrsprachigkeit - Über Herkunft und Vielfalt
  • by Olga Grjasnowa
  • Read: May 24, 2021

The book ended up being a super fascinating read about how language and multilingualism is viewed in Germany. I’ve found myself reminded of a lot of things that I’ve noticed since living abroad in the US and coming back to Germany. And it gave me a new sense of reality of what it means to arrive and live in Germany as someone who’s first language isn’t German and isn’t considered one of the “good” languages either.
