I enjoyed reading this book thoroughly. I’ve been living in Germany almost all of my life and yet most of the things I have read and have influenced my thinking around topics like integration, diversity, migration, race, discrimination, and related topics have been in English and often centered on the US. It was really interesting and good to read a book about the topic in German by a German author who also has the background of being German. It’s a quick but deep and thought provoking book that touches on many topics around how categories are erected to try and tightly define people and their being. Can definitely recommend reading it.
