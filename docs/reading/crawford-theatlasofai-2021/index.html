After reading about the technology of AI I decided to stay with the topic for a bit longer and also read more about the impacts of the technology and its deployment on the world outside of plainly looking at the outputs of the algorithms. Much of it I already knew but the book very clearly looks at the aspects of environmental as well as human and societal impact of these technologies for mass data gathering and processing. It’s very well written and recommended even though it’s not fun to read about this very dark aspect of our current age of technology.
