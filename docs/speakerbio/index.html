Speaker Bio


Daniel Schauenberg is a platform engineer at GitHub. He loves automation, testing, documentation and simplicity. In previous jobs he has worked on building and maintaining deployment systems, infrastructure provisioning, running web services, systems and network administration, on connecting chemical plants to IT systems and as an embedded systems networking engineer. Things he thoroughly enjoys when not writing code include coffee, breakfast, TV shows, reading, and drawing and painting. He maintains a stream of his personal opinions on unwiredcouch.com.
